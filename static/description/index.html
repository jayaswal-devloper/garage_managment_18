Garage Management System

Transform your workshop into a fully digital service center with an advanced Garage Management System. Manage vehicle inspections, service requests, job cards, spare parts, technicians, service history, customer records, and billing operations from a single platform. Improve efficiency, reduce paperwork, and deliver a faster, more professional service experience to your customers.

Key Features

Manage every stage of vehicle servicing with organized workflows and complete service records.

Service Request Management

Register customer service requests and record vehicle issues before starting the service process.

Vehicle Inspection

Capture inspection details and identify required repairs or maintenance activities.

Job Card Management

Create job cards with service details, technician assignments, spare parts, and service charges.

Service Checklist

Ensure all service and inspection points are completed using customizable checklists.

Spare Parts Tracking

Record spare parts used during vehicle servicing and maintain accurate service records.

Service Charges Management

Manage labor charges and service costs associated with vehicle maintenance and repairs.

Technician Assignment

Assign technicians to service jobs and track responsibilities throughout the repair process.

Vehicle Service History

Maintain a complete record of previous services, repairs, and maintenance activities.

Customer & Vehicle Management

Store customer information and vehicle records for efficient service management.

Configuration Flow

Configure the system and prepare your garage operations for daily service activities.

Create A Vehicle Brand

Create vehicle brands with brand logos to build a structured vehicle database and simplify vehicle identification across the garage management workflow.
Vehicle Category Configuration

Create vehicle categories such as Sedan, SUV, Hatchback, Truck, Bus, and Motorcycle to classify vehicles based on their type. This helps organize vehicle records efficiently and simplifies vehicle management throughout the garage workflow.
Create A Vehicle Model & Details

Define vehicle models under their respective brands to accurately record customer vehicles. This helps maintain organized vehicle information and ensures proper tracking of service and maintenance history.
Configure Service Categories & Types

Create service categories such as Maintenance, Repair, Inspection, and Cleaning, along with their respective service types. This helps organize garage services, streamline job card processing, and maintain consistent service records.
Create Service Packages

Create service packages by combining multiple services into a single package. Service packages help offer predefined maintenance plans, simplify service selection, and provide special pricing benefits for customer vehicles.
Configure Package Items

Add products and services to service packages and define individual discount percentages for each package item. This helps create customized maintenance packages with special pricing benefits for customers while ensuring accurate package costing.
Create Service Checklists

Create service checklists to define inspection and maintenance tasks that must be performed during vehicle servicing. Checklists help technicians follow standard procedures and ensure consistent service quality across all job cards.
Create Workshops

Create workshops to manage different garage locations or service centers within the system. Workshops help organize resources, technicians, job cards, and service operations efficiently across multiple work areas.

System Flow

Step-by-step workflow from registration to portal access

Customer Service Request

Faculty selects subject, semester, and sets submission deadline.

Generate Job Card

Confirm the service request to automatically generate a job card for workshop operations.

Vehicle Inspection & Diagnosis

Inspect the vehicle using checklists and record diagnostic findings for required repairs.

Manage Parts & Services

Add spare parts and services, and create sales or purchase documents directly from diagnosis.

Create Work Order

Generate a work order to assign repair tasks and service activities to technicians.

Track Work Progress

Record technician timesheets and track repair progress directly from the work order.

Complete Work Order

Complete all assigned repair tasks and finalize the work order after service completion.

Done Job Card

Mark the job card as done and maintain a complete service history for the vehicle.
